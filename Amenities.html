Jetplex Circle Business Park

Jeplex Circle Business Park offers the following amenities:

  • Secure office and warehouse environment:
    • Interior and exterior lighting
    • Fully fire-sprinkled office and warehouse areas
    • Fully conditioned space available
  • Attractive architectural features.
  • Customizable space:
    • Build-to-suit opportunities
    • Flex-space
    • Generous tenant build-out allowance
  • Fully paved, convenient entranceways and ample parking space for vehicles and people.
  • Convenient access to I-565 and I-65, US Highway 20 and 72, and the Huntsville International Airport. This provides an ideal location for distribution of goods and services to Huntsville, Madison, Decatur, Athens, northern Alabama and southern Tennessee.
  • The Park is located just minutes from Huntsville International Airport. FedEx is adjacent to our Production Avenue location. This proximity to the Airport and this shipper is not only conveient for you, but it will allow you extra time to receive and send products and information to your clients, both domestically and internationally.
  • Fast, convenient access to Huntsville and the tri-cities area, making the movement of people and products easily accessible throughout northern Alabama and southern Tennessee.
  • Quality space in a well landscaped setting. An impressive, yet affordable choice for businesses that require a strategic location.

In addition, our buildings on Production Court are designed to meet the flex-space needs of office/warehouse tenants, and offer the following:

  • Bay Size: 3,300 SF, 30' wide by 110' deep
  • Construction: Tilt-up concrete, which produces an exceptionally secure and attractive building
  • Facade: Attractive architectural design with banding, as seen in the elevations.
  • Entranceways: Attractively designed individual glass entranceways.
  • Attractive landscaping and irrigation.
  • Ceiling Height: 20' clear ceiling height (24" for building E).
  • Dock-High Doors: Each bay has one dock-high door. Each bay also has a rear service door to the courtyard.
  • Drive-In Doors: Buildings D and E each have two drive-in doors on the east and west ends of the buildings.
  • Truck Access: Via dock-high doors and drive-in doors.
