Since 1987, Jetplex Circle Business Park has specialized in serving the office/warehouse needs of companies serving Huntsville, Madison and Northern Alabama. We have some space currently available for lease, are constructing additional buildings, and have additional space planned. We also offer build-to-suit opportunities.

Jetplex Circle Business Park is conveniently located to provide easy access to I-565, I-65, Highway 20 and the Huntsville International Airport. Our central location allows for fast movement of people and products throughout the tri-cities area, northern Alabama, southern Tennessee and the south-central United States. See our Location section for a map and directions.

The convenience of our location is demonstated by the fact that FedEx is adjacent to the Production Avenue location. This proximity improves your next day shipping capability to clients worldwide.
