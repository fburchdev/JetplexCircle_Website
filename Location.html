Jetplex Circle Business Park

Quality Space in a Strategic Location

Jetplex Circle is located just off US 20 (Madison Blvd.) in Madison, AL.

Jetplex Circle is located on Jetplex Circle and Production Avenue, which are just off US 20 (Madison Blvd.) in Madison, AL.

  • From the West: From I-65, take Exit 340 East on I-565. Take exit 5 to Madison Blvd.(AL 20). Turn left on Jetplex Circle or Jetplex Lane to the park.


  • From the East: Take I-565 to Exit 8 at Wall Triana. Go north on Wall Triana to Madison Blvd.(AL 20). Proceed West on Madison Blvd. Turn right onto Jetplex Lane or Jetplex Circle to the park.

The location of Jetplex Circle Business Park (JCBP) on Production Avenue, Jetplex Circle and Production Court provides our multiple tenants an outstanding loction from which to serve their customers in the greater Huntsville and the Mid-South region.

Our tenants have convenient access to I-565, I-65, highways 20 and 72, County Line Road and Huntsville International Airport. The FedEx Air and Ground buildings are located across from JCBP.

We own an additional 8 acres of property, for future development, on Production Avenue immediately across from the FedEx Ground building. Combined with 3 acres of property on Production Court, there are about 11 total acres available for future development.

Jetplex Circle Business Park is an ideal location for service and distribution to the greater Huntsville and mid-south markets.
